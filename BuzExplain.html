What We Do

BusinessConnect SA empowers South African entrepreneurs to sell, list, and raise capital for their businesses, while connecting investors to high-potential opportunities.

1

Sell a Business

Looking to exit? We make it easy to showcase your business to verified buyers across South Africa. Get the best value and ensure a smooth handover process.

2

List a Business

Our listing service allows you to present your business to a wide network of potential buyers and investors. Professional profiles attract serious interest quickly.

3

Sell Equity in Your Business

Don’t want to sell your entire company? Raise capital by selling equity instead. We help you determine valuations, manage investor shares, and close secure deals.

4

Buy Equity

Investors can browse vetted businesses offering equity stakes. Build your portfolio by investing in promising South African ventures.
